The Evolution of Web Standards

HTML5’s Impact

HTML5 represented a major leap forward, meeting the demands for multimedia-rich content and compatibility across devices.

Introduced semantic elements like <article> and <section>, improving content accessibility and search engine visibility.

HTML5's APIs facilitated features like offline capabilities and multimedia embedding without external plugins, which are critical from a cybersecurity perspective.

HTML5 introduced multimedia, gaming, video, and interactive features without plugins.
